FAQs

Do you do a free, no obligation quote?

Yes! To arrange one just contact us.

How can I contact you?

Easy, try the contact form at the bottom of the page to send us a message or use the phone numbers at the top and bottom of the page. If you're on mobile just click the links to call us!

How much do your services cost?

This really depends on what you'd like doing & the size of your property. We are really competitive with our pricing & offer a no obligation free quotation.

I'm not going to be at home when the clean is scheduled, is this a problem?

Of course not! We offer an insured key holding service meaning we can do the clean without you needing to be there. There's nothing like arriving home to a spotless house!

Can I meet with the cleaner beforehand?

Of course! We understand the need to be comfortable with someone whose given access to your house. We'll assign one cleaner to you, and them and only them will remain your cleaner until you choose to end the service.

I've never had a cleaner before, do you provide references / reviews?

Of course! We know it can be very daunting when allowing someone new into your home, so we activly encourage our clients to post reviews on our services on our facebook page.

I have pets, is this a problem?

Not at all, we love animals! We'll get familiar with them and them with us and follow any specific instructions you have whilst we're inside your property.

How can I pay?

Through direct debit, cheque, standing order or cash on a weekly or fortnightly basis.

Is it just domestic homes you clean?

No we do a wide range of commercial properties too! Not sure if what you have in mind is something we can help with? Get in touch and we'll do our best to accomodate you!

I'm allergic to certain cleaning products, how does this affect the service?

We will tailor the clean to your exact needs, and that includes using products that are suitable to your allergies!

We're happy to answer any other questions you may have!
